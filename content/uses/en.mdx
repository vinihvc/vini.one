- **AirPods 2 Pro** -
  Great sound quality and noise cancellation.

## Coding

- **[Cursor](https://www.cursor.sh/)** -

- **[Warp](https://www.warp.dev/)** -
  Blazing fast terminal.

- **[JetBrains Mono](https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/)** -
  The font I use for coding. It has ligatures and it looks great.

- **[Monokai Night](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=fabiospampinato.vscode-monokai-night)** -
  My favorite theme. I tried others, but I always come back.

  Minimalistic icons. They are easy to recognize.

## Apps

- **[Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/firefox)** -
  My browser of choice. Fast, private, and no chromium like.

- **[Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com)** -
  My password manager of choice. Open-source and free.

- **[Raycast](https://www.raycast.com/)** -
  An app launcher that I use to open apps, run scripts, and much more.

- **[Kap](https://getkap.co/)** -
  A simple and free screen recorder. I use it to record my screen.

- **[NextDNS](https://nextdns.io/)** -
  A DNS resolver that blocks ads and trackers.

- **[Apple Mail](https://www.icloud.com/mail)** -
  I use Apple Mail for my personal and work emails. It works great.

- **[Shottr](https://shottr.cc/)** -
  A screenshot and annotation tool for macOS.

- **[Pocket Casts](https://pocketcasts.com/)** -
  The best podcast app. I use it every day.
